# Chicagoland Assisted Living Directory — Base Data (40 facilities)

> This is the canonical seed dataset for the directory site. It covers Chicago +
> five collar counties (Cook, DuPage, Lake, Will, Kane). Every entry follows the
> facility schema in `references/facility-schema.md`. Figures marked as aggregator
> estimates and any license number should be re-verified against the live IDPH
> LLCS database (llcs.dph.illinois.gov) before publishing. Supportive Living
> Program (SLP) facilities are certified by Illinois HFS, not IDPH-licensed, so
> they will not appear in the IDPH assisted-living license database.

## Region-level facts (reusable in intros)

- Average cost of assisted living in Chicago: ~$7,040/mo (CareScout 2024 Cost of
  Care Study, cited via Caring.com / U.S. News) — ~$1,140 above the national
  median ($5,900) and ~$1,204 above the Illinois median ($5,836). Chicago is the
  second-priciest Illinois metro after Decatur (~$7,875). Memory care typically
  runs meaningfully higher than assisted living.
- Two regulatory tracks: (1) Standard assisted living licensed by IDPH under the
  Assisted Living and Shared Housing Act (210 ILCS 9/1 et seq.; regs at 77 Ill.
  Admin. Code Part 295; licensing began July 2002). (2) The affordable/Medicaid
  Supportive Living Program (SLP) administered by Illinois Healthcare and Family
  Services (HFS) under 89 Ill. Adm. Code 146; SLFs are certified, serve
  elderly/disabled Medicaid-waiver beneficiaries under a 1915(c) waiver, with
  service payment based on 60% of the average regional nursing-facility rate.
- Recurring well-reviewed operators: Belmont Village, Sunrise Senior Living,
  Brighton Gardens (Sunrise), LCS/Life Care Services, The Arbor Company,
  StoryPoint, and Gardant (largest AL provider in Illinois; 5th largest
  nationally per Gardant).
